Photos
Every image is a self-portrait, even when the photographer is nowhere in frame.

Overview

I’ve been taking photos for years, but only began sharing them publicly in 2022. I’m not a professional photographer—just someone who loves the process. I hope you enjoy viewing these images as much as I enjoyed capturing them.

With the exception of two, all of these stills were taken with a range of iPhones (the oldest being a 11 Pro Max and the newest a 15 Pro). I color graded them with the Photoleap App for the iPhone.

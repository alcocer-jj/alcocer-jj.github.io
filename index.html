Hi and welcome! I am currently a third-year doctoral student at the University of Southern California’s Department of Political Science and International Relations. Here, my primary areas of academic research include legislative institutions, political communication, race and ethnicity, social media and politics, and computational social science. Prior to joining the program, I completed my undergraduate studies and obtained my Bachelor's degree in Political Science at California State University, Long Beach and then moved on to obtain my Master of Public Policy at the University of Southern California’s Sol Price School of Public Policy–where I specialized in Applied Econometrics, Health, and Environmental Policy. Outside of academia, I enjoy programming, learning new languages, traveling, and amateur photography. I have shared some of the pictures that I have taken over my travels (like the one on this page above) in the Photography section, hoping they provide some cool amusement and inspiration.
